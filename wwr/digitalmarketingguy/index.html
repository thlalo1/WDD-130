Social Media Management

Social media management involves analyzing social media audiences and developing strategies that are tailored to them. This includes creating and distributing content for social media profiles, monitoring online conversations, collaborating with influencers, providing community service, and monitoring, measuring, and reporting on social media performance and ROI.

Customer Relations

Customer relationship management (CRM) is the set of tools, strategies and technologies used by businesses to manage and analyse customer interactions and data over the customer lifecycle. The objective is to improve customer service relations, help with customer retention and encourage sales growth.

Lead Generation

Lead generation is the process of acquiring potential customers' interest in order to increase future sales. It is a crucial component of the sales process for numerous companies. A lead is anyone who has displayed interest in a company's products or services but may not yet be qualified to purchase.
